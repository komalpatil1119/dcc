Investor Onboarding

A Clear and Disciplined Investor Onboarding Process

Our onboarding process is designed to ensure strategic fit, efficient diligence, and structured engagement from initial interest to investment evaluation.

01

Initial Contact

Investor submits interest through direct outreach or a request for an investor/project brief.

02

Investor Briefing

Relevant opportunity note, sector summary, and high-level structure details are shared.

03

Fit Assessment

Sector interest, capital appetite, ticket size, mandate alignment, and strategic fit are reviewed.

04

Structured Engagement

Detailed project discussions, structure review, diligence process, and execution pathways are initiated.

05

Onboarding & Next Steps

Qualified investors proceed into deal evaluation, documentation flow, and formal onboarding steps.
